Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Sweet Success: The Journey of Cones & Cream Ice Cream Parlor

Cones & Cream was born out of a deep love for ice cream and a desire to share that love with the world. Our founders, Jane and John, have been passionate about ice cream since they were kids, and always dreamed of opening their own ice cream shop. After years of planning and hard work, their dream became a reality when they opened the doors to Cones & Cream. From the beginning, they knew they wanted to create a place where people could come together to enjoy the simple pleasures of life - good company and even better ice cream. At Cones & Cream, we believe that ice cream has a special way of bringing people together. Whether you're enjoying a cone with friends, treating your family to a sundae, or just taking a moment to savor a solo scoop, our goal is to make every experience with our ice cream as sweet and memorable as possible.

Specialty Ice Cream Cakes

Ask us about our custom made cone cakes.

Strawberry Swirl Cone Cake

Soft vanilla ice cream swirled with fresh strawberry puree, sandwiched between light and fluffy cake layers, topped with strawberry flavored waffle cone.

Banana Chocolate Dream Cake

Layers of chocolate and banana ice cream sitting between layers of decadent chocolate cake. Decorated with banana macaroons, chocolate ganache, and chocolate dipped waffle cone.

Rainbow Bright Cone Cake

Delicious layers of bright purple blueberry ice cream, sunny yellow lemon cake, green mint ice cream, orange sherbet, and rich and creamy pink strawberry ice cream on top.
