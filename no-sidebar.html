Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Sweet Success: The Journey of Cones & Cream Ice Cream Parlor

At Cones & Cream, we believe that ice cream has a special way of bringing people together. Whether you're enjoying a cone with friends, treating your family to a sundae, or just taking a moment to savor a solo scoop, our goal is to make every experience with our ice cream as sweet and memorable as possible.
We use only the finest, freshest ingredients to create our delicious flavors, and we're always experimenting with new recipes and combinations to keep things exciting. Our team of dedicated ice cream enthusiasts is passionate about creating the best possible experience for our customers, from the moment they step through our doors to the last lick of their cone.
We're proud to be part of the community here at Cones & Cream, and we're excited to share our love of ice cream with everyone who walks through our doors. Come visit us and let us treat you to a scoop of happiness!

Specialty Ice Cream Cakes

Ask us about our custom made cone cakes.

Strawberry Swirl Cone Cake

Soft vanilla ice cream swirled with fresh strawberry puree, sandwiched between light and fluffy cake layers, topped with strawberry flavored waffle cone.

Banana Chocolate Dream Cake

Layers of chocolate and banana ice cream sitting between layers of decadent chocolate cake. Decorated with banana macaroons, chocolate ganache, and chocolate dipped waffle cone.

Rainbow Bright Cone Cake

Delicious layers of bright purple blueberry ice cream, sunny yellow lemon cake, green mint ice cream, orange sherbet, and rich and creamy pink strawberry ice cream on top.
